What you get with Linux Mative Unity

Linux Mative Unity is a modern Ubuntu-based build designed for those who appreciate the convenience and aesthetics of the Unity desktop while wanting a current, fast, and thoroughly optimized system without unnecessary limitations. Unity has elements such as the heads-up display (HUD) and the Global Menu, helping with your productivity. It has a modern-looking user interface, but with an elegant, traditional workflow.

The distribution completely removes and blocks Snap, preventing it from being reinstalled. Instead, it uses Flatpak — a flexible, controllable packaging format that gives you full freedom in choosing applications.

The system ships with Firefox in .deb format, installed directly from Mozilla’s official repository, ensuring stability, performance, and independence from Snap.

Linux Mative Unity combines an updated Unity experience, modern tooling, enhanced optimizations, and a reliable architecture — preserving the classic workflow and making it truly comfortable on both old and new hardware.
